  [1XAbstract[101X
  [33X[0;0YThe  [5XGAP[105X  package  [5Xglabella[105X provides a low level interface to softwares that
  can  compute automorphisms, isomorphisms and canonical labellings of graphs.
  The  graphs  can  be  directed  or undirected, and a vertex colouring can be
  specified.  In  the  present version, the package provides interfaces to the
  programs [13Xbliss[113X and [13Xnauty[113X.[133X
